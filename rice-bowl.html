Viral TikTok Salmon and Rice Bowl

Description

This Salmon and Rice Bowl is a TikTok viral sensation, it’s so simple to make using leftover rice and salmon! Serve with Japanese mayo, soy sauce, diced avocado, and roasted seaweed sheets!

If you’ve been on TikTok recently, you’ve probably seen mounds of videos recreating the viral salmon rice bowl from TikTok creator, Emily Mariko. She made a Salmon Rice Bowl using left over salmon and rice, and an ice cube( that’s right!), soy sauce, Sriracha, Japanese Kewpie mayo and, to serve, sliced avocado, kimchi and roasted seaweed snacks. After watching her video, I knew I needed to recreate it and I’m here to tell you it’s worth the hype!

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillet
  • Leftover rice
  • Soy sauce
  • Sriracha
  • Kewpie mayo
  • Roasted seaweed sheets
  • Avocado
  • Kimchi
